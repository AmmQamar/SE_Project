Amusement Park Management system

This project is all about managing a whole amusement park and its working parts on a single software. This Management system takes all the important component of an Amusement park and put them in one place so anyone who is coming in the park as a customer or as a staff member can use it and get all the important info on their mobile or laptop screen.

Customer:
For customers, you can use this software to get all the info related to rides and arcade games as well the other entertainments like food court and gift shops in your pocket instead of roaming around and looking for helping desk.
As a customer, you can have access tokens and tickets section where they can find the brief info regarding the prices and ticket selection. Customers get any ride and play any game using their unique token number. The user can buy tickets online and then use the ticket when he is in the park. Another feature is customer care. The users can ask for their queries and file complaints regarding the park management via email.
