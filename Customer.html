Park Managemnt system(Customer side)

This project is all about managing a whole amusement park and its working parts on a single software. This Management system takes all the important component of an Amusement park and put them in one place so anyone who is coming in the park as a customer or as a staff member can use it and get all the important info on their mobile or laptop screen.

Booking

                 Are you tired of standing in long queues for tickets and tokens? Then don't worry, we got your back. With this feature, you can book a ticket or tokens for your favourite ride and arcade games from your mobile. All you have to do is select the details of the ride, the number of tickets, add your buying details, confirm and voila! you got your tickets with a single click. You can also book the whole ride for yourself to make your experience even more memorable with your loved ones.
